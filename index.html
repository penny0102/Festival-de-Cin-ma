Discription

The CinéPleinAir festival is a free outdoor film event held in a friendly and warm atmosphere. Over three days, visitors can enjoy screenings of classic cult films, meet passionate directors, and take part in film critique workshops. More than just a film festival, CinéPleinAir offers a unique experience combining cinema, music, and food through dedicated food truck areas and live musical entertainment. It is the perfect opportunity to share unforgettable moments with friends or family.
